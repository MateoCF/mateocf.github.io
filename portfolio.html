The Doomscroll Machine

Marshall McLuhan coined the phrase that 'the medium is the message', and The Doomscroll Machine exemplifies this phrase in a modern context. This website invites users to watch any YouTube video as a scrollable feed to mediate on how a medium's interactivity shapes the perception of it's content. Superficial like, comment, and share buttons included.

doomscrollmachine.com

The Difficulty Collective

The Difficulty Collective is an collection of personal experiences and friends’ anecdotes that illustrate why friction and effort is important in a society obsessed with efficiency and optimization.

Each post caption contains questions such as "How do we impose our values into and onto ‘neutral’ tools?" and "What do we gain -or lose- from such 'easiness'? ".
